What's This All About?

Hi, I'm Dane. I've started around 16 businesses and taught over 2,000 people how to start a successful business when the odds are not good.

No ideas. Limited time. Limited cash. Lots of self talk. Doubt. And crippling fear.

Because that was me, at 22, scammed with $123 to my name.

I believe in building equity in everything I do. I don't easily exchange time for money. So I work hard once and set up an income stream for a long time.

In the 16 different businesses, 11 failed, 5 succeeded. The 4 that are currently active are PaperlessPipeline, AgentCareCenter, RecruitingNinja, and The Foundation.

Of the 11 that failed, I came up with those ideas. LOL.

Of the 5 that succeeded, those ideas were given to me by finding pain using variations of "The 5 Questions".

Then I created The Foundation.

After teaching thousands of entrepreneurs and watching multi-millionaires get created - I was feeling sad that I wasn't being rewarded with that equity. I took time away from my business to teach - which is hard fucking work - that I don't get paid for in the same way if I start my own business. It left me feeling poor.

But I love helping people. The pain of not helping people left me searching for an alternative.

So out of that pain, I decided to create this Business Idea Network where I could collect equity in successful entrepreneurs in exchange for mentorship.
